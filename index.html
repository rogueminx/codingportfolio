About Anna-Marie

Current Work

I am currently working as a self-employed clothing designer. I have my clothing line called ROGUE:MINX that specializes in women's clothing. Stylistically, it is a mix of dark rock and roll with just a splash of bohemian. I have recently move more toward lingerie design, and I am curious about getting more involved with designing my own fabric prints and moving into swimwear.

Why Coding?

  1. General curiosity - My current work doesn't involve much technology, and I'd like more variety, and the chance to learn new things.
  2. Flexibility - You can find jobs in tech in most cities, and it could even allow you to work remotely.
  3. Useful - Learning more about coding and tech may be useful for my business in the future. It could help me with obvious things like website building, and maybe even lead me into firguring out ways to make the clothing industry more efficent and less wasteful.
